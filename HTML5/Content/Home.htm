Frequently Asked Questions

What is MadCap Flare?

MadCap Flare is a powerful tool designed for technical writing, allowing users to create, manage, and deliver high-quality documentation efficiently. It is commonly used by technical writers and content developers to produce content that can be published in multiple formats, including HTML, PDF, Microsoft Word, and eBooks.

What is the Calendar Application?

The examples on this site are based on the Google Calendar application. Google Calendar is a versatile, cloud-based tool designed to help individuals and teams efficiently manage their schedules and keep track of events, appointments, and deadlines. This free time-management and scheduling application, developed by Google, allows users to create and manage events, appointments, and reminders in a digital calendar format. Google Calendar is accessible on the web and on mobile devices through dedicated apps for Android and iOS.
